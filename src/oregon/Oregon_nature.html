Famous nature-made sites

Thor's Well

Thor's Well, located along the rugged coastline of Cape Perpetua in Oregon, is a natural wonder that captivates visitors with its dramatic display of nature's power. This spectacular sinkhole appears to drain water directly into the ocean, creating a mesmerizing vortex effect, especially during high tide and stormy weather. Often referred to as the "Gate to the Underworld," Thor's Well offers a thrilling and awe-inspiring sight, drawing photographers and nature enthusiasts to witness its breathtaking beauty against the backdrop of the crashing waves and rocky cliffs.
