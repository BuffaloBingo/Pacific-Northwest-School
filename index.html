About the Pacific Northwest

The Pacific Northwest(PNW), aka Cascadia is a region in North America consisting of Oregon, Washington, and British Columbia. The PNW is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, and at the heart of its natural allure lie its lush and expansive forests. It also offers the sight of the Cascades and Coastal range. Though there is tons of nature in the PNW, there are also remarkable urban areas. Some famous urban areas in the PNW include Seattle, Portland and Vancouver(BC).

Main States/Provinces in the PNW:

  • Oregon, USA
  • Washington, USA
  • British Columbia, Canada

Northern California is usually considered to be part of the PNW, and sometimes even Idaho. However, they are not included here as Idaho is only sometimes considered and Northern California is much smaller than the other states/provinces in the PNW.
